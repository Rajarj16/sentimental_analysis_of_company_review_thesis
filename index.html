Model Architecture & Preprocessing
RQ1 · RQ2 · RQ3 · Objective 2 · Objective 3 · Objective 4
Model 1 — RoBERTa-base · RQ2
✦ 12-layer bidirectional transformer · 125M params
✦ BPE tokeniser · max seq 512 tokens
✦ Classification head: Linear + Dropout + Softmax
✦ AdamW · LR 2e-5 · Warm-up 10% steps · Grad clip 1.0
✦ Staged: frozen encoder (3 ep) → full fine-tune (7 ep)
✦ Solo Macro F1: 0.871
Model 2 — Bi-LSTM
✦ GloVe 300d (Wikipedia 2014 + Gigaword 5)
✦ 2 stacked Bidirectional LSTM layers
✦ 256 units/direction → 512-dim bidirectional states
✦ Spatial dropout p=0.3 between layers
✦ Global max-pooling → FC(256, ReLU) → Softmax
✦ Adam · LR 1e-3 · Batch 64 · Class-weighted loss
✦ Solo Macro F1: 0.842
